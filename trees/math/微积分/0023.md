title: 有理四次不可约
taxon: example

$\gdef\spaces#1{~ #1 ~}$
$\gdef\d{\operatorname{d}}$
$\gdef\Q{\mathbf{Q}}$
$\gdef\R{\mathbf{R}}$

为了处理如下这种分母为高次 $\Q$ 不可约多项式的积分, 我们可以考虑其在 $\R$ 上的分解 $x^4+1=(x^2+\sqrt{2}x+1)(x^2-\sqrt{2}x+1)$, 因为系数 $\sqrt{2}$ 并不影响我们积分. 

$$ \int \frac{1}{1+x^4} \d x \spaces= \frac{1}{2\sqrt{2}} \int \bigg(\frac{x+\sqrt{2}}{x^2+\sqrt{2}x+1} - \frac{x-\sqrt{2}}{x^2-\sqrt{2}x+1} \bigg) \d x $$

但此方法不易计算, 我们观察: 

- $I = \int \frac{\d x}{1+x^4}$, $J = \int \frac{x^2}{1+x^4} \d x$, $I+J$ 和 $I-J$ 是容易计算的, 因为 $$ \begin{aligned} I+J &\spaces= \int \frac{1+\frac{1}{x^2}}{x^2+\frac{1}{x^2}} \d x \spaces= \int \frac{\d(x-\frac{1}{x})}{(x-\frac{1}{x})^2+2} \spaces= \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\tan^{-1}\frac{x-\frac{1}{x}}{\sqrt{2}} \end{aligned}$$ $$ \begin{aligned} I-J &\spaces= -\int \frac{1-\frac{1}{x^2}}{x^2+\frac{1}{x^2}} \d x \spaces= -\int \frac{\d(x+\frac{1}{x})}{(x+\frac{1}{x})^2-2} \spaces= -\frac{1}{2\sqrt{2}}\log\frac{x+\frac{1}{x}-\sqrt{2}}{x+\frac{1}{x}+\sqrt{2}} \end{aligned}$$ 解得 $$ I \spaces= -\frac{1}{4\sqrt{2}} \log \frac{x^2-\sqrt{2}x+1}{x^2+\sqrt{2}x+1} + \frac{1}{2\sqrt{2}}\tan^{-1} \frac{x^2-1}{\sqrt{2}x} + C$$ $$ J \spaces= \frac{1}{4\sqrt{2}} \log \frac{x^2-\sqrt{2}x+1}{x^2+\sqrt{2}x+1} + \frac{1}{2\sqrt{2}}\tan^{-1}\frac{x^2-1}{\sqrt{2}x} + C $$

- 也可以考虑 $I = \int \frac{x^2+1}{1+x^4}\d x$, $J = \int \frac{x^2-1}{1+x^4} \d x$, 于是 $\square = \frac{I-J}{2}$. 现在 $ I = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\tan^{-1}\frac{x-\frac{1}{x}}{\sqrt{2}}$, $J = \frac{1}{2\sqrt{2}}\log\frac{x+\frac{1}{x}-\sqrt{2}}{x+\frac{1}{x}+\sqrt{2}}$, 故 $$ \square \spaces= \frac{1}{2\sqrt{2}}\tan^{-1}\frac{x^2-1}{\sqrt{2}x} -\frac{1}{4\sqrt{2}} \log \frac{x^2-\sqrt{2}x+1}{x^2+\sqrt{2}x+1} + C $$
